BLAST

The basic local alignment search tool (BLAST) enables comparison of a query protein sequence with a database of sequences. Accordingly, sequences that share regions of similarity with the query are identified. BLAST searches in Homo sapiens, Mus musculus, Caenorhabditis elegans and Drosophila melanogaster identified a conserved Ras domain spanning the majority of H-Ras. This domain is responsible for hydrolysis of GTP to GDP.
